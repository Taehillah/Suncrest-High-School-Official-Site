RE-OPENING IN 2024

School will re-open officially on Wednesday the 17 of January 2024. Learners will be given the opportunity to pay the first installment of school fees and receive textbooks if not done so already in December.

School Fees (2024)

Annual school fees for 2024 are as follows:
1st Child: R9 950.00 (R950.00 resource fee, R900.00 x 10 months)
2nd Child: R9 450.00 (R950.00 resource fee, R850.00 x 10 months)
3rd Child: R8 950.00 (R950.00 resource fee, R800.00 x 10 months)

If the full amount is settled before 31st March 2024, a discount of one month's school fees may be deducted from the total.
Please be reminded that all accounts that are not settled in full by 30th November 2023, without justification, will be declared as "bad debt" and legal proceedings may be initiated to recover debt.

Cash Free Zone

As from 2024, Suncrest will be a cash free zone which means school fees will only be paid through EFT, Bank deposit or Debit order. Very Important upon payment: School Reference: Learner's Name and Grade!!! Until further notice, the Grade for the Learner will be the grade the learner was in 2023. If this is not correct, we cannot allocate the amount to the correct account.

Dress Code (Boys)

Light blue shirt,
Long grey pants/or shorts and tie.
Long grey pants must be long enough to cover socks and shorts must be a proper length (fingers above the knee).
No altering of pants allowed.
Pants are not allowed to be rolled up, or hem shortened for ankles to be visible, and no alteration to narrow pants that it looks like skinny pants.
Long or short sleeved blue shirt with proper collar and
blazer or jersey allowed to be rolled up.
BLACK school belt. ONLY GREY SOCKS ALLOWED
Only black school shoes allowed. (No boots - or any shoe covering the ankle.)
NO earrings, tongue rings, nose rings, eyebrow studs, necklaces, bracelets, or any other form of jewellery to Boys must be CLEANLY shaven during all times. (This includes beards, sideburns, and moustaches) - due to any medical reasons for boys being unable to shave, a Di's note will be required.
No shaving of eyebrows in any pattern

Dress Code (Girls)

Light blue shirt, grey slacks/or claret skirt and tie.
Grey slacks/claret skirt - skirt must be four fingers above the knee when standing, shorter hem must be taken down straight away. Skirts should not be altered or made narrower.
The skirt must not be shorter than the blazer. Maroon or black ski pants must always be worn under the skirts (No other colour ski pants allowed). White or grey socks. White socks to be worn with skirts and white or greys socks with pants.
Plain black stockings allowed through the year. (No designer stockings).Black or white Socks can be worn underneath stockings. Jewellery is not allowed. Girls may wear a single gold or silver stud or small sleeper in each ear and must be worn in the bottom hole. NO diamonds, pearls etc. NO nose, tongue or eyebrow studs are allowed. NO bangles, bands, or necklaces Coloured nail polish is not allowed. Nails should be short and clean. (Nail is not allowed to be longer than the nailbed) No make up to be worn including lip gloss or lip liner. (Coloured of Natural)

Hair Rules

BOYS GIRLS
a. Boys' hair must be short, neat, and combed. a. Girls' hair always needs to be neat and tidy and suitable for school purposes.
b. NO PATTERNS allowed TO BE CUT INTO HAIR. b. Long Hair has to be nicely tied up at ALL times.
c. Length of the hair IS not allowed to be longer than comb 4 machine guide. c. No patters allowed to be cut into natural hair.
d. No dye on forehead must be visible. d. Girls are not allowed to colour their hair.
e. No shaving patters on the eyebrows allowed. (If done eyebrows to be shaved off) e. No wigs allowed to be worn.
f. When facial hair (moustache and/or beard) becomes visible, it must be shaved.
Age does not determine when a boy must start shaving. Boys must be always clean-shaven.
Boy's not cleanly shaven must be referred to the office for immediate shaving before allowed back in class 		f. Hair is not allowed to touch the collar of the shirt if so, it should be tied in a neat bun.
